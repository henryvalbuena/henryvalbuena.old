Welcome

My name is Henry and I am driven by simple curiosity, since my adolescence I have been into computers, how they work and what makes them do what they do. Then, I went to the university in search for knowledge and expertise about electronics, is there where in my first course of programming I fell in love with one of my favorite hobbies, coding. Since then, I have been working as a professional in my engineering career and in my free time building programs, learning how to use Java, C/C++ and others programming lenguages just for fun. To me, it was unreal to just understand how to create something that worked in my computer and did something incredible as well. With time my passion was only growing, it was then at the beginning of 2018 when I decided to make a career change to build software and pursue my passion as a professional.

How cool it can be to do what you love every day and also get paid for it... right?
